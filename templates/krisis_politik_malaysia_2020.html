TOPIC:
Resolution of 2020's Malaysian Political Crisis

It is a peculiar time in the history of the Federation. The gears of the Dewan Rakyat have run its course, for politicking has intensified to levels previously unimaginable. Perhaps the political scene of the country shall change. Perhaps the very fate of Malaysia shall be changed.


One cannot forget the course that Pakatan Harapan desired to take ever since their tremendous electoral victory in 2018. It was said that Tun Mahathir Mohamad, the veteran statesman now elected Prime Minister, shall hand over the prime ministership to no other than Anwar Ibrahim. Yet, something went wrong. As internal strife gripped Pakatan Harapan over this very issue, Muafakat Nasional and its dependents have sensed an opportunity. Through internal politicking and backdoor deals, perhaps the government may be toppled. It is here that Pakatan Harapan and Muafakat Nasional shall stand at each other’s throats.


It is also here where you, Yang Berhormat Members of Parliament, shall stand. It is here where you shall decide the fate of Malaysia. Perhaps Pakatan Harapan will stand strong, perhaps Muafakat Nasional will retake its lost hegemony. It will be up to you, and you only.


Note: This night council shall have specialized mechanics in order to facilitate the dynamism of the political crisis that it represents. Start date of this council will be the 22nd of February 2020.
