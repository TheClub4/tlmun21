Payment Details

The details required to make the payment will be emailed to you once you have successfully registered your name through the links above.

Please be informed that your allocation into the conference can only be processed once the payment has been made. The payment is required to be made at least 14 days after receiving the confirmation email or at least 2 days before the first day of the conference, whichever comes first. If the payment is not made within that time frame, your delegate allocation will be forfeited and you will be required to re-register to participate.

The link to the respective council Discord server will be emailed to delegates at least a week before the conference after the payment has been made. An e-certificate will be given to all delegates who have fully participated in the conference unless a valid reason is given for not doing so.

Refund Policy

Thank you for your interest to join TLMUN 2020. However, we understand the potentiality of any unprecedented event interdicting your participation in the conference. The eligibility of receiving a refund will be stated as below.

A notification to cancel participation must be received by TLMUN Finance Department via email not later than 7 days after completion of payment or 2 days before the day of the conference, whichever comes first to be eligible for a refund. Please indicate the adequate payment verification such as a bank transaction screenshot for our reference and recording purposes in the email. Cancellations requested exceeding the period of time stipulated above will not be entitled for a refund.

Cancellations due to medical conditions or any other unforeseeable circumstances indubitable beyond one's control will be subjected to our review on a case-by-case basis. We are able to allow full refunds for delegates who successfully provide reasonable and sufficient verification of such circumstances whatsoever before 12/02/2021

Please send in your refund request by email Email : finance.tlmunconference@gmail.com Email Heading : Payment Refund Note : Kindly attach payment verification for our reference.

After requesting for a refund, you will receive an update within 3 working days. If your refund is approved, refunds of fees are typically issued via your original payment mode. An email notification will be sent to you once your refund is processed. In the event you did not receive any responses within the specified period of time (3 working days) , please do not hesitate to contact us at finance.tlmunconference@gmail.com

Note: Merchant fees from the initial transaction and other bank transfer feed will be deducted from the amount refunded.
