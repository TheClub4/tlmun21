Meet the Secretariat

The Secretariat is a ten-person team tasked with running the entire conference. From overseeing the tens of staff members to liaising with the hundreds of delegates that attend the conference, it is their leadership that makes TLMUN the most well-run, prestigious Model UN conference in Malaysia.

Secretary Generals

Lim Ke Wen

Lim Ke Wen

SECRETARY-GENERAL

Young Jun Andrew Kim is a junior at Harvard College in Eliot House concentrating in Economics, and is originally from Seoul, South Korea. He has previously served as Director of the South Korean Women’s Presidential Advisory Board in HNMUN 2020 and is thrilled to be leading the Operations organ as an USG in HNMUN 2021. In HMUN, he has served as an Assistant Director of Administration and Director of Business. Outside of Model UN, Andrew is a member of Harvard’s Catholic Student Association and works part-time as a campus tour guide. In his free time, he enjoys traveling, plane-spotting, and napping.

He looks forward to helping make this year’s HNMUN the best one yet!
