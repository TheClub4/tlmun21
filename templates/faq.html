Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

TLMUN is going virtual! If you do not find the answer to your question below or on this website, please feel free to contact us at info@tlmun.org.

  • Do I have to be located in Malaysia to participate in TLMUN?

    Not at all! Each year, over half of our delegates come from Malaysia, and we expect to exceed that number this year. While TLMUN will be held on a Singapore Standard Time (SGT) schedule, we are working to provide a schedule that enables all to participate, regardless of whether or not they are located in Malaysia. TLMUN's only requirement for participation is that all delegates are currently enrolled in a college or university.
